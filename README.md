# 🍔 Elemes.id

> Modern food delivery platform for ordering meals online.

---

## ✨ About

Elemes.id is a modern food delivery website that allows users to discover restaurants, browse menus, and order food online quickly and conveniently.

The platform connects customers with local restaurants and provides a simple ordering experience through an intuitive interface.

---

## 🍕 Services

- 🍔 Online food ordering
- 🥗 Restaurant browsing
- 📋 Digital menus
- 🚚 Food delivery tracking
- ⭐️ Customer reviews and ratings
- 💳 Online payment options

---

## 🌟 Features

- 📱 Responsive design
- 🔍 Search and filter restaurants
- 🛒 Shopping cart functionality
- 📦 Order tracking
- ❤️ Favorite dishes and restaurants
- 🌐 Multi-page navigation

---

## 🧭 Website Structure

- Home page
- Menu page
- Restaurant page
- Food categories
- About page
- Contact page

Users can easily navigate between pages using internal links and the navigation menu.

---

## 🎯 Goals

- Simplify online food ordering
- Connect customers with restaurants
- Improve delivery convenience
- Provide a smooth user experience
